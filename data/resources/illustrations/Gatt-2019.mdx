## Abstract

This blog post by Heritage Gatt explores the integration of manual and digital techniques in 3D archaeological illustration. Published on Sketchfab, the article discusses how traditional hand-drawn illustrations can be combined with digital 3D modeling to enhance archaeological documentation and interpretation. The post provides practical insights into how these two approaches complement each other, offering new ways to visualize and communicate archaeological data.

## Purpose and Audience

This blog post is aimed at archaeologists, illustrators, and digital heritage professionals interested in combining manual drawing methods with 3D digital modeling techniques. It offers practical examples and tips for integrating both techniques in archaeological projects.
