## Abstract

This book explores the techniques and methods for accurately drawing flaked stone artifacts. It provides guidance on tools, materials, and processes that enhance illustrations' quality for academic publications. The book is structured to be useful for both professional archaeologists and students, providing detailed step-by-step instructions on how to represent lithic artifacts visually in a scholarly context.

## Purpose and Audience

This book is designed primarily for archaeologists and illustrators who specialize in lithic artifact depiction. It's also useful for students and professionals in related fields who are interested in enhancing their technical drawing skills for archaeological artifacts, especially those preparing illustrations for publication.

## Key Excerpt

"Mastering the techniques of lithic illustration requires not just artistic skill but an understanding of the archaeological context in which these artifacts are studied. This dual knowledge ensures that illustrations are both scientifically accurate and aesthetically appropriate for publication."
