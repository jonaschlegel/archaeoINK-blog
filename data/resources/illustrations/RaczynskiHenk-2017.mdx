## Abstract

_Drawing Lithic Artefacts_ by Dominika Raczynski-Henk provides a detailed guide to illustrating lithic artifacts, focusing on the principles and techniques required to accurately depict stone tools for archaeological research and publication. Published by Sidestone Press, this book combines practical drawing instructions with a discussion of the role and significance of illustrations in archaeological documentation.

## Purpose and Audience

This book is intended for archaeologists, illustrators, and students who are involved in documenting and studying lithic artifacts. It provides a comprehensive overview of the methods used to create accurate and detailed drawings of stone tools for academic and professional purposes.

## Table of Contents

1. Introduction to Lithic Illustration
2. Tools and Techniques for Drawing Stone Tools
3. Depicting Flakes, Cores, and Other Lithic Artefacts
4. Shading and Detailing Techniques
5. Case Studies in Lithic Illustration

## Key Excerpt

"Accurate and detailed illustrations of lithic artefacts are essential for the study of prehistoric technologies. Drawing these objects requires not only artistic skill but also a deep understanding of the tool’s form and function."
